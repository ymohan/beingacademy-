By Whom

Learning is the nature of life.

Naturally, there is no teaching of any sorts. Awakening the learner is the thrust of the program and whoever are part of this program have been involved in their own exploration and they will only share their experience without any attempt to teach or convince.

Naturally one has to learn from oneself !

By observing oneself, Familiarizing, Exploring the language trap, Breaking cognitive habits, Awakening senses, Awakening the sense of process, Awakening the sense of aesthetics, Learning from materials, Learning from Nature, Learning from the illiterates, Learning from children.
