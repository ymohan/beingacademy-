The Space

The Being Academy is being imagined/ evolved/ designed to create condition for regaining our capacity to use senses, to experience the world, to be present, to regain the wonder, innocence, trust, the playfulness, to be spontaneous, genuine, authentic, to be in awe with the world to be in the world.

This means creating conditions for becoming a learner, for re-claiming qualities, attitudes, skills needed to learn the real world. The external space is only a means and it is the internal space that matters finally
