Program

The 15-month program is divided into three stages. During the first 6 months all participants will be in Raisen near Bhopal, an organic farm (themadmansfarm.org), the next 6 months the participants will be mentored by people who are already working in the field in various nature friendly activities. The last three months the participants will be back to Raisen to collate, reflect, document and share their experiences and to mentor the next batch.

ARRANGEMENTS

Simple dormitory accommodation. On the principle of self-management- organizing food, cooking, cleaning etc. – an ashram like life. Simple vegetarian food. We expect the expenses (including the stay and food) during the first six months to be around Rs 500 per person per day. The fees for the six months’ stay with selected mentors will be based on actual expenses
